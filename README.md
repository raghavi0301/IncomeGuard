IncomeGuard AI
Predict • Protect • Stabilize Income for Delivery Workers

1. Understanding the Problem:
Food delivery partners working with platforms like Swiggy and Zomato depend completely on daily orders to earn their income. But in reality, their income is very unpredictable. A sudden rain, extreme heat, or even pollution can reduce the number of orders and slow down deliveries.
On such days, workers don’t just face inconvenience — they earn much less than usual. Sometimes their income drops by 20–30% in a single day. Right now, there is no proper system that helps them deal with this kind of uncertainty, So the real problem is not just disruption, it is unstable income.
 
2. Our Idea:
We are building IncomeGuard AI, a platform that focuses on keeping a worker’s income stable, even when disruptions happen.
Instead of behaving like traditional insurance, our system:
    - predicts disruptions before they affect work
    - gives simple alerts to workers
    - and automatically compensates income loss
So the goal is not just to pay money, but to reduce and recover loss.

3. What Makes Our Idea Different:
Most solutions think like this:
      Disruption → claim → payout
But we thought a little differently.
What if we could help workers prepare before the disruption happens?
	Our system works like this:
    Prediction → awareness → adaptation → compensation
This means:
•	workers get early alerts
•	They can adjust their work timing
•	And still get support if they lose income


4. Who Are We Building This For?
We are focusing on Food delivery partners (Swiggy / Zomato).
For example:
Rahul is a delivery partner in Chennai.
He usually earns around ₹600 per day by completing deliveries.
His income depends on how many orders he gets and how fast he can deliver them.

5. A Simple Real-Life Situation:
	On a normal day:
   Rahul earns ₹600
	On a rainy day:
    He earns only ₹250
So his income loss is:
₹600 – ₹250 = ₹350

Our system helps in two ways:
- it alerts Rahul earlier about the risk
- it compensates part of the loss automatically
So instead of a bad day, it becomes a manageable day

6. How the System Works?
1. The worker registers in the app
2. The system learns their average daily income
3. The worker selects a weekly plan
4. The system keeps checking weather and environmental data
5. AI predicts possible disruptions
6. If disruption happens, income drop is detected
7. A quick fraud check is done
8. Compensation is automatically sent
The idea is to make everything simple and automatic for the worker.

7. Weekly Protection Model:
We follow a weekly model because gig workers usually think in weekly earnings.
Plans are simple:
- Basic → ₹20/week
- Standard → ₹30/week
- Premium → ₹40/week
Workers don’t have to think every day — they stay protected for the whole week.

8. When Does the System Trigger:
Instead of just checking numbers like rainfall, we focus on impact.
Examples:
- Heavy rain → fewer orders
- Heatwave → shorter working hours
- Pollution → unsafe conditions
 So the real trigger is:
   “Can the worker earn normally today?”

9. How We Calculate Insurance?
Step 1: Average Income
We calculate the worker’s normal earning using past data:
Average Income = total earnings (last 14 days) / 14
Example:
₹600 per day

Step 2: Income Loss
If disruption happens:
•	Expected income = ₹600
•	Actual income = ₹250
•	Loss = ₹350


Step 3: Compensation
We cover a part of the loss (around 70–80%)
Example:
₹350 × 0.8 = ₹280
This makes the system fair and realistic.

Step 4: Premium Calculation
Premium depends on:
- worker’s income level
- risk in their area
- selected plan
Example:
o	Base = ₹20
o	High risk = +₹10
o	Final = ₹30/week

10. Where We Use AI?
AI helps in:
- predicting disruptions before they happen
- estimating expected income
- detecting income drops
- identifying fake claims
 AI is not just for data, it helps in decision-making.

It helps with:
- quick onboarding

- easy tracking
We will also have a simple web dashboard for monitoring.
